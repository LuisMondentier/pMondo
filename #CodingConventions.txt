~Classes~
Every class' name word on caps: (for example: BartSimpson, Bistro)
public members on lowercases separated with underscores: (for example: bart_simpson, bistro)
private members on lowercases starting with underscores, the second word starts on caps: (for example: _bartSimpson, _bistro)
methods on lowercases separated with underscores (for example: bart_simpson, bistro)
